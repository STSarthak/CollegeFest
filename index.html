AIM-2-GAME

A competition that will test the gaming skills of the participants as well as entertain them with the thrill and frills of gaming. It is a place where gamers can come and evaluate themselves. There will be two games called COUNTER-STRIKE GO (first-person shooter video game) and NFS-MOST WANTED (racing video game).

TREASURE HUNT

The participants should have good thinking capability and they should be quick enough to figure out the clues given in form of IT riddles to finish the game in shortest span of time.

CODE Breaker

Participants should have a thorough knowledge and understanding of the concepts and should possess an insight to write the code in either C/ C++/Java. The algorithm for the code snippet would be provided by the coordinators. The participants also get the opportunity to showcase their brilliance by understanding the algorithm. Each algorithm will be tested based on the critical test data. The students would be judged on the basis of their coding skills.

TAP-APP

Tap App is a platform for students to display their talent in Android development. Participants will be allowed to use only the provided software i.e. Android Studio. The teams will be provided three simple tasks where each task will comprise of an Android application to be built. The applications will be tested on the basis of design and functionality. In case of a tie, the team with maximum running applications in the minimum time will be declared winner.
