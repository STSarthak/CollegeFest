TREASURE HUNT

SKILLS REQUIRED

The participants should have good thinking capability and they should be quick enough to figure out the clues given in form of IT riddles.


General Rules

  1. Registration open to any college student enrolled in undergraduate or graduate level course.
  2. Participants have to present college id-proof for registration.
  3. On the spot event registration till fixed time.
  4. Two participants per team are allowed.
  5. All teams have to nominal registration fee for registration.
  6. There will be three rounds in total.
  7. Rules for each round will be shared before the round starts.
  8. Teams have to keep the clues with them until the game finish.
  9. Each qualified team should play the whole game.
  10. Team cannot quit the game in between the rounds.
  11. Members of the team should not get separated and the team members should remain together throughout the game in JIMS campus.
  12. Decision taken by the judges will be final and binding.
  13. Total time for the event would be 2 hrs.
  14. Each team would be given a colour band for identification throughout the game.
